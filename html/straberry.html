strawberries!

Strawberries are an easy to grow fruit crop that will reward the home gardener with ample harvests for many years. With favorable combinations, each strawberry plant should produce one quart of strawberries.

Where to Plant Strawberries

Basic considerationswhen locating a strawberry patch include

  • full sun
  • well drained, sandy loam with a soil pH from 5.8 to 6.2 is ideal
  • Don't plant where tomatoes, potatoes, peppers or eggplant have been grown recently

Enjoy strawberries! They are good for you!
